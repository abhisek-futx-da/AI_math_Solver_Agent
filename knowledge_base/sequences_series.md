# Sequences and Series (JEE Level)

## Arithmetic Progression (AP)
- General term: aвӮҷ = a + (nвҲ’1)d
- Sum of n terms: SвӮҷ = n/2 В· [2a + (nвҲ’1)d] = n/2 В· (a + l)
- Arithmetic mean of a and b: AM = (a+b)/2

## Geometric Progression (GP)
- General term: aвӮҷ = arвҒҝвҒ»В№
- Sum of n terms: SвӮҷ = a(1 вҲ’ rвҒҝ)/(1 вҲ’ r), r вү  1
- Sum of infinite GP (|r| < 1): SвҲһ = a/(1вҲ’r)
- Geometric mean of a and b: GM = вҲҡ(ab)

## Harmonic Progression (HP)
- 1/a, 1/(a+d), 1/(a+2d), ... is HP if a, a+d, a+2d, ... is AP
- Harmonic mean of a and b: HM = 2ab/(a+b)

## Relationship: AM вүҘ GM вүҘ HM (for positive numbers)

## Binomial Theorem
- (a + b)вҒҝ = ОЈ C(n,r) aвҒҝвҒ»Кі bКі   (r = 0 to n)
- General term: TбөЈвӮҠвӮҒ = C(n,r) aвҒҝвҒ»Кі bКі
- C(n,r) = n! / (r!(nвҲ’r)!)
